## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes governance for managing information security risks associated with third-party suppliers who access or process CodeVolt information.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All suppliers with access to CodeVolt information
- Suppliers providing information processing services
- Cloud service providers and hosted solutions
- Professional service providers

---

## 3. Governance

### Policy Owner
The Information Security Manager owns this policy and ensures:
- Supplier security requirements are appropriate
- Supplier assessments are conducted
- Compliance is monitored

### Procurement
Procurement processes incorporate information security requirements for supplier selection and contracting.

---

## 4. Supplier Risk Assessment

Suppliers are categorized by risk level based on:
- Sensitivity of information accessed
- Criticality of services provided
- Volume of data processed
- Degree of access to systems

Risk assessment informs the level of security assurance required.

---

## 5. Security Requirements

### Selection Criteria
Supplier selection considers:
- Security certifications and compliance
- Security track record and reputation
- Ability to meet contractual security requirements
- Data protection capabilities

### Contractual Controls
Supplier agreements address:
- Information security responsibilities
- Data protection obligations
- Incident notification requirements
- Right to audit where appropriate
- Secure termination and data return

---

## 6. Ongoing Management

### Monitoring
Supplier security is monitored through:
- Service level monitoring
- Security performance reviews
- Incident tracking and reporting
- Periodic reassessment

### Change Management
Changes to supplier services or arrangements are assessed for security impact before implementation.

---

## 7. Review

This policy is reviewed:
- At least annually
- When supplier landscape changes significantly
- Following supplier-related security incidents
