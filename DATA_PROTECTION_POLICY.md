# CodeVolt Data Protection Policy

**Effective Date:** February 3, 2026  
**Review Date:** May 3, 2026

---

## 1. Data Collection Principles

CodeVolt collects personal data in accordance with these principles:
- **Lawful and transparent** — Data collected with clear notice and consent
- **Purpose limitation** — Used only for stated purposes
- **Data minimisation** — Only necessary data is collected
- **Accuracy** — Kept accurate and up to date
- **Limited retention** — Kept only as long as necessary

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Newsletter and Communications
- Email address
- Subscription timestamp
- IP address (for security purposes)

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Names or physical addresses
- Phone numbers
- Payment information
- Browsing behavior or tracking data
- Third-party profile information

---

## 3. Purposes and Legal Basis

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Email | Deliver newsletters and product updates | Consent |
| Timestamp | Maintain accurate consent records | Legal obligation |
| IP Address | Security monitoring and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- Data stored on secure servers within the UK
- Encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption
- Access restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular backups maintained with encryption

### Security Measures
- File system permissions restrict access
- Encryption for data at rest and in transit
- Regular security assessments
- No use of third-party marketing services

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Active subscriber data | Until unsubscribe request received |
| Unsubscribed user data | 30 days from unsubscribe date |
| Failed delivery records | 7 days |
| IP address logs | 90 days (then anonymised) |
| Backup data | 90 days |

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your personal data.

### Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### Objection
Object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

### Restriction
Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

## 8. Data Sharing

CodeVolt does not:
- Sell personal data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers or marketers
- Use data for purposes beyond those stated
- Transfer data outside the UK/EU without safeguards

---

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

CodeVolt uses only essential cookies necessary for website functionality. We do not use:
- Tracking cookies
- Analytics cookies
- Advertising cookies
- Third-party tracking services

---

## 10. Data Breach Response

In the event of a personal data breach:
- Immediate containment measures implemented
- Assessment of breach scope and impact
- Notification to affected users within 72 hours when required
- Documentation and reporting to supervisory authorities as required
- Implementation of preventive measures
