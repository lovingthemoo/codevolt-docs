## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the organization's commitment to information security and provides the foundation for the Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO 27001:2022.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All CodeVolt information assets
- All employees, contractors, and third-party users
- All locations where CodeVolt information is processed
- All systems, networks, and services

---

## 3. Governance and Leadership

### Senior Management Commitment
Senior management demonstrates leadership and commitment to the ISMS by:
- Ensuring information security policy and objectives are established
- Integrating ISMS requirements into business processes
- Ensuring resources are available for the ISMS
- Communicating the importance of information security
- Supporting continual improvement

### Policy Owner
The Information Security Manager is responsible for:
- Maintaining this policy and associated documentation
- Reporting on ISMS performance to senior management
- Coordinating information security across the organization
- Ensuring compliance with applicable requirements

---

## 4. Information Security Objectives

CodeVolt maintains objectives for information security that:
- Are consistent with the information security policy
- Are measurable (where practicable)
- Consider applicable requirements
- Are monitored and communicated

Objectives address:
- **Confidentiality** — Protecting information from unauthorized disclosure
- **Integrity** — Maintaining accuracy and completeness of information
- **Availability** — Ensuring authorized access when required

---

## 5. Risk-Based Approach

Information security risk management is integral to the ISMS:
- Risk assessments identify threats and vulnerabilities
- Risk treatment selects appropriate controls
- Residual risks are acceptable to senior management
- Risk assessments are reviewed periodically

---

## 6. Security Principles

CodeVolt's approach to information security is guided by these principles:

1. **Security is everyone's responsibility** — All personnel contribute to security
2. **Defense in depth** — Multiple layers of protection implemented
3. **Least privilege** — Minimum necessary access rights granted
4. **Need to know** — Access based on legitimate business requirements
5. **Continuous improvement** — Regular review and enhancement of security

---

## 7. Roles and Responsibilities

### Information Security Manager
- Overall security strategy and governance
- Policy development and maintenance
- Incident response coordination
- Compliance monitoring and reporting

### System Administrators
- Implementation of technical security controls
- Vulnerability and patch management
- Backup and recovery operations
- Access control administration

### All Users
- Adherence to security policies and procedures
- Prompt reporting of security incidents
- Protection of authentication credentials
- Responsible handling of information assets

## 9. Compliance

CodeVolt maintains compliance with:
- ISO 27001:2022 — Information Security Management Systems
- UK GDPR — Data Protection
- Data Protection Act 2018
- Applicable legal and contractual requirements

Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action.

This policy is reviewed:
- At planned intervals (annually minimum)

Changes to this policy are communicated to relevant personnel.
