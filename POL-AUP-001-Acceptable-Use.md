**Version:** 1.0

**Approved By:** Senior Management

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of CodeVolt information systems and resources to protect organizational assets and ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All users of CodeVolt information systems
- All CodeVolt-owned or managed resources
- All access methods and locations

---

## 3. Governance

### Policy Owner
The Information Security Manager owns this policy and ensures:
- Users are aware of acceptable use requirements
- Violations are addressed appropriately
- Policy remains relevant and effective

### User Responsibilities
All users are responsible for:
- Reading and understanding this policy
- Using resources in accordance with this policy
- Reporting suspected violations

---

## 4. Authorized Use

CodeVolt resources may be used for:
- Authorized business purposes
- Activities supporting organizational objectives
- Approved personal use within defined limits

---

## 5. Prohibited Activities

### Security Violations
Users shall not:
- Share authentication credentials
- Bypass access controls or security measures
- Install unauthorized software
- Disable or circumvent security features

### Data Misuse
Users shall not:
- Access information without authorization
- Copy sensitive data to unsecured locations
- Transmit confidential information via unsecured channels
- Exfiltrate organizational data

### System Misuse
Users shall not:
- Use resources for unlawful purposes
- Excessively consume resources for non-business purposes
- Introduce malicious software
- Attack or probe systems without authorization

---

## 6. Monitoring

CodeVolt reserves the right to monitor system usage to:
- Ensure compliance with this policy
- Protect organizational assets
- Support incident investigation

Monitoring is conducted in accordance with applicable laws and privacy requirements.

---

## 7. Violations

Policy violations may result in:
- Immediate suspension of access
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal proceedings where appropriate
- Reporting to authorities when required

---

## 8. Reporting

Suspected violations should be reported to rook@codevolt.co.uk. Reports will be investigated confidentially.

---

## 9. Review

This policy is reviewed annually or when operational requirements change.
